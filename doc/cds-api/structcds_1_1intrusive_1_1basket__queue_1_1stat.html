template<typename Counter = cds::atomicity::event_counter>
struct cds::intrusive::basket_queue::stat< Counter >

Basket queue internal statistics. May be used for debugging or profiling.

Basket queue statistics derives from cds::intrusive::queue_stat and extends it by two additional fields specific for the algorithm.
